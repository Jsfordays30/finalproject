About the Warriors

Championships: The Warriors have a rich history of success, particularly in recent years. They have won a total of six NBA championships, with their most recent titles coming in 2015, 2017, and 2018. These championships were achieved during a period known as the "Golden State Warriors dynasty." Steph Curry: Stephen Curry, often referred to as Steph Curry, is the face of the Warriors franchise. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest shooters in NBA history. Curry has won multiple MVP (Most Valuable Player) awards and has been instrumental in the team's success. Splash Brothers: The term "Splash Brothers" refers to the dynamic backcourt duo of Steph Curry and Klay Thompson. Both players are renowned for their exceptional shooting skills and have formed one of the most lethal shooting tandems in NBA history. Oracle Arena to Chase Center: The Warriors played their home games at the Oracle Arena in Oakland for many years. In 2019, they moved to the Chase Center, a state-of-the-art arena located in San Francisco, which has become the new home of the team.
